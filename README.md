
# 📦 Supply Chain Analysis

## 🔍 Problem Statement

Analyze supply chain data to understand sales performance, inventory levels, logistics efficiency, and profitability, and provide actionable insights for business improvement.

---

## 🛠 Tools Used

* Excel (Data Cleaning & Preprocessing)
* Power BI (Data Visualization & Dashboarding)

---

## 📁 Dataset

* Sales and order data containing product, customer, logistics, and revenue information

---

## 🧹 Data Preparation

* Cleaned raw data in Excel by removing blanks and fixing inconsistent formats
* Structured dataset for analysis and visualization

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

* Monthly sales trends (Line charts)
* Region-wise performance analysis (Bar charts)
* KPI cards for revenue, orders, and profit
* Product category and SKU performance
* Logistics and transportation cost analysis

---

## 📈 Key Insights

### 🛍 Product Performance

* Skincare products lead in both sales and stock availability
* Haircare and cosmetics show lower sales but higher stock → overstock issue

### 💰 Revenue Analysis

* Skincare contributes ~42.8% of total revenue
* Female customers generate the highest revenue share

### 📦 Inventory & SKUs

* Top 10 SKUs generate balanced revenue (~9.3K–9.7K)
* Haircare & cosmetics dominate top SKU positions

### 🚚 Logistics & Delivery

* Supplier 5 has fastest delivery (2.37 days)
* Supplier 4 is slowest (3.06 days) → needs improvement

### 🌍 Regional Performance

* Kolkata leads in profit and order volume
* Bangalore and Delhi show lower performance

### 💸 Transportation Cost

* Rail & road transport are most expensive
* Sea transport is most cost-efficient

---

## 🚀 Conclusion

The analysis highlights strong performance in skincare products and metro markets, while identifying opportunities to optimize inventory, logistics, and supplier efficiency.

---

## 📌 Recommendations

* Optimize inventory to reduce overstock in low-performing categories
* Improve logistics efficiency to reduce delivery time and cost
* Focus on high-performing regions for expansion
* Strengthen supplier coordination to improve lead time

---

## 📊 Project Outcome

This project demonstrates how data analysis and visualization can support better decision-making in supply chain management.
